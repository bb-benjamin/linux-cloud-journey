# Week 2 — Networking Fundamentals

This week was all about understanding how the internet actually works under the hood.
Not just the theory — but what is really happening every time you open an app on your phone.

---

## What I learned

- **IP Addressing and CIDR** — how every device on the internet gets its own address, and how addresses are grouped into ranges
- **DNS** — the internet's phone book that turns names like `google.com` into numbers computers can use
- **HTTP and HTTPS** — how data travels between your phone and a server, and why the S matters for security
- **Firewalls** — the security guards that decide what traffic is allowed in and what gets blocked
- **NAT** — how internal servers hide behind one public address
- **Load Balancing** — how traffic gets spread across multiple servers so nothing crashes under pressure
